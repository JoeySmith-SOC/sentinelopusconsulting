What I Do

I engineer, automate, and secure digital and physical infrastructure — bridging cybersecurity, identity, and operational technology.

Identity & Access Management (IAM)

I architect and maintain enterprise-grade identity solutions with Okta, Active Directory, and SAML/OIDC. My focus: lifecycle automation, access governance, and strong authentication workflows that integrate cleanly with infrastructure.

Zero Trust Architecture

I design and implement Zero Trust networks using Cloudflare Access, role-based policy, and certificate-based authentication. Every connection is verified, logged, and encrypted — without adding friction to users or operations.

Infrastructure & Automation

I automate infrastructure provisioning and security baselines with Ansible, Terraform, and PowerShell — delivering repeatable, version-controlled environments that align with DevSecOps principles.

Operational Technology & Electrical Security

I bridge IT and field engineering — hardening SCADA/ICS networks, segmenting PLC systems, and integrating cybersecurity into electrical infrastructure. My goal: protect systems where code meets current.
